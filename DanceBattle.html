"Dance Battle - Dance your way to the top" is a 2D RPG where you dance to defeat your enemies. The game is fully controllable with a dance mat, making for a unique experience.

The game was created as part of the GameLab 1 project at the Julius-Maximilian-University of Würzburg.

Using: Unity, C#
