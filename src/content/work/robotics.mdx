For my Scientific Assistant job at the Chair for Robotics I assisted with the hardware and software development of a mobile robotic platform for the autonomous supervision of solar panels.
I assisted my with the development of hardware and software components, helping with the 
wiring of the components, creation of 3D print files and the development of the mapping and navigation
system.
