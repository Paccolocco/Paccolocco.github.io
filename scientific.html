Julius-Maximilians-Universität Würzburg

As part of my work at the University of Würzburg I'm working on PePUT, the Pepper-Python-Unity Toolkit. PePUT is an application that allows the user to easily connect any amount of Pepper Robots to a Unity application. Pepper can then be controlled and tracked over Unity, allowing the user to integrate Pepper into games and other interactive applications. PePUT is currently still in development but due to release soon, allowing other universities to conduct research with Pepper more easily.
