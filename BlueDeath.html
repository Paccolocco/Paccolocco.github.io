Dance Battle was created as a combination of role play gaming and dance to elevate the dance mat as a tool out of it's intended genre, thus showing how the use of alternative game controllers is not limited to their original sub-genre. To achieve this Dance Battle was conceptualized and developed to be fully controllable with a dance mat without making the alternative control scheme confusing or irritating for the player
